Homemade Lasagna


Facts about Lasagna!

Lasagna hails from Italy during the 14th century.
This food was introduced to italians during the middle of the Black Plague.
Lasagna was introduced to the US by Italian immigrants in the late 19 century


List of Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound of ground pork
  • 1/2 pound of ground beef
  • One onion diced
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs: chop 2 tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic
  • teaspoon of sugar
  • dried basli
  • dried oregano
  • salt and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles from any brand
  • cottage cheese
  • shredded mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned. Add the onion and keep cooking until the onions are translucent
  2. Add the canned tomatoes, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoon of salt, oregano, and suger them stir
  3. cook the noodles by boiling them in lightly salted water until they are al dente
  4. Mix the cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, salt, and black pepper in a bowl
  5. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, then sauces, and ending with cheese until the lasagna is assembled
  6. cover with foil and bake for about half and hour then remove the foil and bake until top is golden brown
  7. serve and enjoy!
