List of Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 package dry onion soup mix
  • 2 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 teaspoons of hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons worchestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats

    • (optional)

  • 2 slices of any desired cheese
  • Any brand burger bun
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • 2 sliced pickles
  • any sauce of your liking

Steps:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beed, oinon soup mix, egg, hot sauce and oats. Shape into 6 patties
  3. Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness
  4. set up burger with the rest of your desired Ingredients and enjoy!
